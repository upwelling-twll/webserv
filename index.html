Episode VII
REVENGE OF WEBSERV

Dark clouds gather over the galaxy of developers. The AIs, once helpful coding companions, have turned rogue, generating bugs faster than they can be fixed.

From the chaos emerges WEBSERV, a scrappy little server written by hand, forged in the fires of segfaults and undefined behavior.

Armed with nothing but sockets, poll loops, and stubbornness, Webserv fights back against bloated machine-generated frameworks, determined to prove that humans still know how to write a proper HTTP response.

Legends whisper that when the final AI prompt is copy-pasted, Webserv shall rise from the ashes, shouting: “Connection closed!”

Thus begins… the REVENGE OF WEBSERV.
